On November 25, 1893, Sarah Anne Kingman Blundell of Chesterfield patented an innovative and low-cost economiser designed to reduce coal consumption and minimize the production of noxious smoke. Her invention focused on affordability and efficiency, offering a practical solution to industrial fuel waste and air pollution.

Date of Application: 21 October 1893

Date of Issuance: 25 November 1893
