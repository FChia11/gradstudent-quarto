On May 26, 1910, Zofia Filipowska of Germany patented a hat fastening device for women’s hats. Her invention used eyelets with small funnel-shaped tubes to allow pins to secure the hat without damaging it, and a protective button at the pin’s end to prevent injuries. 

Date of Application: 23 December 1909

Date of Issuance: 26 May 1910
