On May 1, 1950, Lillian Julie Lyhr of Copenhagen patented a method for removing blackheads using tweezers with rounded rubber jaws. Her design allowed impurities to be safely extracted without injuring either the patient or the beautician.

Date of Application: 3 April 1948

Date of Issuance: 1 May 1950
