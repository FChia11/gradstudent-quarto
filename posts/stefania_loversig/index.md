On March 24, 1934, Stefania Loversig of Argentina was awarded a patent for a novel fuel-gasifying system for internal-combustion engines. Her invention used exhaust heat to convert heavy fuels into gas and mechanically linked all valves to the accelerator pedal, allowing automatic switching between light and heavy fuels.

Date of Application: 29 September 1933

Date of Issuance: 24 March 1934
