On March 21, 1905, Jenny Proskauer of St. Louis, Montana, was awarded a patent for a novel fork. It combined straight tines for holding slices of bread with curved tines designed for lifting other foods, like meat.

Date of Application: 23 January 1905

Date of Issuance: 21 March 1905
