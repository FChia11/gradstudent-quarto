On September 3, 1907, Abby C. Lemon of Roanoke, Virginia, received a patent for an improved dumping wagon. Her design featured a tilting wagon body with a locking tailboard and removable lifting mechanism that made unloading and loading heavy materials easier and more efficient.

Date of Application: 14 May 1907

Date of Issuance: 3 September 1907
