On April 21, 1903, Arrah J. Whisler of Newark, New Jersey, received a patent for a seamless toy balloon. She shaped the balloon on an oval mold so that, when inflated, it naturally became round instead of flattening at the ends.

Date of Application: 18 February 1903

Date of Issuance: 21 April 1903
