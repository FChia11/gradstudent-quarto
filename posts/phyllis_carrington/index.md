On July 31, 1923, Phyllis Carrington of New York patented a women’s sandal featuring padded soles and woven, paired strands designed to secure the foot comfortably. These interlaced strands formed a flexible yet stable structure that prevented slipping while remaining easy to put on and remove.

Date of Application: 16 February 1922

Date of Issuance: 31 July 1923
