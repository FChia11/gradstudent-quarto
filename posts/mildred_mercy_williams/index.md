On August 29, 1896, Mildred Mercy Williams, a gentlewoman from Dulwich, London, received a patent for a new type of children’s teething pad. Her design used a porcelain or glass shape with rubber-covered ends, making it safer and less likely to break.

Date of Application: 9 June 1896

Date of Issuance: 29 August 1896
