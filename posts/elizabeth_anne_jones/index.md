On March 9, 1895, Elizabeth Anne Jones, a spinster from Dudley, UK, was awarded a patent for inventing a novel golf bag. The bag featured a built-in support to prevent the clubs from falling over without an attendant.

Date of Application: 9 March 1894

Date of Issuance: 9 March 1895
