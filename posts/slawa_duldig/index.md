On August 15, 1934, Slawa Duldig of Vienna, Austria, patented a padded device designed to make carrying skis on the shoulder more comfortable. Her invention used a waterproof rubber casing filled with soft sponge rubber to create a light, springy cushion, reinforced where it meets the skis and secured with an adjustable strap for easy attachment and removal.

Date of Application: 6 February 1934

Date of Issuance: 15 August 1934
