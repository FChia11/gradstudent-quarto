On October 27, 1942, Ana Bosi de Fallardi of Buenos Aires, Argentina, patented an improved liquid container with an integrated spout designed for clean and controlled pouring. Her design prevented liquid on the container’s top from flowing back inside and could accept a removable dispensing tube for easier use.

Date of Application: 19 June 1939

Date of Issuance: 27 October 1942
