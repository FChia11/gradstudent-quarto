On October 11, 1910, Minnie Averill of Joplin, Missouri, was granted a patent for her novel toy beehive. Her invention featured a case with an upper part that included several flexible springs designed to hold toy bees when opened. 

Date of Application: 16 July 1909

Date of Issuance: 11 October 1910
