On September 18, 1941, Λιλας Κών / Νως of Athens, Greece, patented a novel machine to process toilet paper into smaller rolls. Her device would guide and pressure rollers along with a pair of interlocking cylinders to create evenly spaced perforations in the paper.

Date of Application: 17 September 1941

Date of Issuance: 18 September 1941
