On July 15, 1947, Albertina Kaegi of Zurich obtained a patent for a circular cooking-vessel accessory that used a rotating scraper to loosen food from the sides. The scraper was guided along the rim and designed with enough flexibility to adapt to slight imperfections in the vessel’s shape.

Date of Application: 5 June 1946

Date of Issuance: 15 July 1947
