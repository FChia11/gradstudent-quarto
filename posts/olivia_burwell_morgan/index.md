On October 22, 1940, Olivia Morgan Burwell of Phoenix, Arizona, received a patent for a foldable bed screen to protect sleepers from insects and animals. The design fit standard bed frames, had rounded corners for safety, and could be adjusted for partial or full coverage.

Date of Application: 29 August 1939 

Date of Issuance: 22 October 1940
