On May 31, 1948, Maria Adriana Rotteveel-Landman of The Hague, Netherlands, patented a portable photocopying device — a compact, flat machine with a moving light source that allowed users to easily copy documents and book pages anywhere.

Date of Application: 3 February 1947

Date of Issuance: 31 May 1948
