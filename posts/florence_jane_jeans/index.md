On November 11, 1893, Florence Jane Jeans, a primary school teacher from London, UK, was awarded a patent for a new way of using "signpost" letters and numbers to mark the correct boxes on chequered paper. Her invention made it easier for children to follow lines and learn effectively.

Date of Application: 7 October 1893  

Date of Issuance: 11 November 1893
